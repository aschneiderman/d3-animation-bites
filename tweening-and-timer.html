Animation Experiments: Custom Tween and Timer

What are experiments revealed was that for the kinds of animations we want to do,
using custom tweening or the timer isn't the way to go -- largely because we don't want
to have to worry about whether the particles are colliding with each other and
how close they are to each other. But just in case we end up with other needs,
here is what we learned.
